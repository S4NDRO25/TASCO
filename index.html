-- Our Services --

Supply & Installation

Our team of highly skilled technicians offer a comprehensive supply and installation service, working with you to determine the best and most cost-effective solution for your needs. We only use high-quality materials and equipment, ensuring your system is built to last.

Maintenance

We offer a comprehensive maintenance service covers all aspects of your system, including regular cleaning, filter replacement, refrigerant checks, and inspecting electrical connections to ensure your air conditioning or refrigeration system is operating efficiently and effectively, helping to reduce energy consumption and save you money.

Service & Repair

Our repair service covers a range of issues, from faulty compressors to refrigerant leaks and broken thermostats. Our experienced technicians provide a prompt and reliable repair service for urgent issues, ensuring your system is back up and running as quickly as possible

Application Installation

Our team of highly skilled technicians have years of experience in power and appliance installation. We offer a comprehensive installation service for a wide range of electrical equipment. We work efficiently to minimize any disruption to your home or business, and we guarantee that all installations will comply with the latest safety standards and regulations.

Media & Data

Our team of expert technicians are experienced in media and data installation and can provide you with the latest technology for your home or business. We offer a range of media and data solutions, including home theatre installation, TV and antenna installation, structured cabling, and much more.

Maintenance & Repair

Regular maintenance and repair are essential to keep your electrical system running smoothly and safely. We offer a comprehensive maintenance and repair service that includes everything from minor repairs to major system upgrades. We use the latest equipment to diagnose and repair a wide range of issues. We also offer emergency repair services for urgent issues.
